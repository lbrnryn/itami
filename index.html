About Us

Welcome To Zebedee Travel & Tours





Zebedee Travel and Tours is the best travel agency in Binangonan, Rizal. Founded in 2007 celebrating more than 8 years in the travel industry, ZBDT&T has a proven track record of providing the highest quality professional travel services in a convenient office location at 0603 National Rd., Brgy Calumpang Binangonan, Rizal. We have an awesome team of 7 people who provide all of our client needs. We were named “God’s Gift Travel Agency” by our happy and satisfied costumer, where it derived its name Zebedee (Greek word) for God’s gift. As highly respected company in the travel industry, ZBDT&T is affiliated and accredited with various travel related entities. These include the National Association of Independent Travel Agencies, National Statistics Office, Department of Foreign Affairs, Bureau of Immigration, Rizal Travel Agencies Association Inc. and Binangonan - Associations of Tourism Entrepreneurs. ZBDT&T meets the demand of travel related services through efficient use of information technology. ZBDT&T is powered by Global Travel Distribution Systems (GDS) namely Amadeus System, Sqiva Reservation System, VIA, BEDSONLINE giving it total control over booking reservations for airlines, hotels, rail services, car rentals, travel insurance and many other travel related services.
